Jiaqi Ye received the B.Eng. degree from the Huazhong University of Science and Technology, 
Wuhan, China, in 2015, and the M.Res. and Ph.D. degrees from the University of Birmingham, 
Birmingham, U.K., in 2018 and 2022, respectively. Notably, Jiaqi is the only Engineering PhD 
student that successfully passed their PhD viva with “no corrections” in 2022.

His academic journey has encompassed a broad spectrum of electronic, electrical, and systems 
engineering. Currently, he works as a Research Fellow at the esteemed Autonomous Systems Lab 
within the School of Engineering, University of Birmingham.
